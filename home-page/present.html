2008: Glitch Game Testers

Glitch Game Testers is a reserach project in Atlanta, Georgia, with the aim to expose young Black high school boys to computer science and game development through early-beta testing of Cartoon Network's game FusionFall (2009, Cartoon Network).

2009: Resident Evil 5

Resident Evil 5 is a third-person shooter game developed and published by the Japanese video game company, Capcom. It takes place in a fictional region of West Africa. However, the game found controvery for its use of Black zombies and white male protagonist.

2026: Black History Month Game Jam

On websites like itch.io, game jams are hosted hourly, weekly, or longer. Within the Black History Month Jam, games were intentionally created to archive and honor Black History in America. Games include puzzle-slides of Marcus Garvey and more.

The subaltern of Africa ard already seen as zombies in many ways in that they are not only regarded but also depicted as disenfranchised mobs viewed in the West only in the context of footage of protests, and civil war” (Geyser, Tshabalala, 12).
